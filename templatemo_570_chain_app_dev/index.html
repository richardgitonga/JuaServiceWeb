How Jua Service Works

A service provider dials *483*063#ok and Opts In to Jua Services he or she provides and their location. Once registered customers request for services offered by the service provider within the same area, the service provider is notified via sms. The service provider issues a quotation and once the customer accepts the bargain contact details are shared for more business engagements.
